Vehicles

Our vehicles are state of the art, ensuring the safety of all future explorers. Becuase each of our journeys varies widely in length, each takes place on a different spaceship.

The Archimedes

The Archimedes is the premiere spaceship of our fleet used during the Intragalatic Tour. The Archimedes is 1345 meters long featuring Archimedes guest suites—ten first class, twenty business, and fifty economy—, a pool, a shopping mall, a arcade, and a skating rink. The Archimedes is the fastest ship in our fleet, boasting a 30,432 kmph travel speed and making the roundtrip journey between Earth and the first four planets in our solar system in only 48 weeks.

The Defiant

The Defiant is our fleet's flagship, serving as the vessel for our lunar journeys. The Defiant is 1630 meters long with a speed of 28,167 kmph. As the Defiant serves travelers going to and from the moon, it does not have suites, instead featuring airline-like seating. It has fifteen first class, thirty business class, twenty economy-plus, and one hundred economy seats. The total time in the Defiant from takeoff to lunar landing is thirteen hours.

The Independece aka "The Death Star"

The Independece is the newest ship in our fleet created to enhance passengers' experience during our Martain Journey. With explorer suites, a small shopping mall, and an athletic complex, the Independece is 1776 meters in length. It has a total of seventy-five explorer suites: ten first class, fifteen business, and fifty economy. As our newest ship, the Independece is capable of going faster than every other ship, a whopping 46,051 khmp!
