Safety

In order to ensure the safety of all of our clients, we require all future explorers to go through a safety training semina. In addition to this seminar, all future explorers go through a rigorous six-month long training regiment. At the end of this regiment, every explorer is required to pass the end of course exams to gain flight clearance. For more information, please visit our training information page.
