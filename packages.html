The Moon

Overwhelmed by the size, weather, and overall complexity of the galaxy? Then the moon is right for you!
1/4th the size of earth and pretty predicatable as far as weather goes, the moon keeps it classic and cool. (Literally! Did you know that the moon can be as cold as -153 Degrees Celcius in areas of shade?)

Pricing

Worried about how much a trip to the moon costs? A round trip ticket to the moon and back is only $15,000.

Details

On your trip to the moon you will:

  • Go on a one hour guided moonwalk across the Kepler Crater!
  • Visit the landing site of Apollo 11!
  • Go shopping in the Mall of the Moon at Armstrong Station!
  • Visit the Alan B. Shepard Museum of Lunar Art!

Mars

Curious about the Red Planet? Mars, our closest celestial neighbor, is a beautiful planet ripe for exploration. As the closet planet in size and shape to our own, Mars is the best planet to visit for new galatic explorers.

Pricing

This all-inclusive roundtrip to Mars is $40,000.

Details

On your trip to Mars you will:

  • Go on a one hour, guided expedition across the Columbia Hills complex!
  • Visit the first agricultural center on Mars, where Earth life was first transplanted to another planet!
  • Explore the galaxy's first galatic settlement, New Terra!

Intragalatic Tour

Can't choose which planet you want to visit? Then our Intergalatic Tour is for you! Out intergalatic tour is our most exclusive package. Explorers on this tour will get to experience what life is like on the other three rocky planets of our solar system: Mercury, Venus, and Mars. The intergalatic tour also makes a stop on the moon!

Pricing

As befitting of our most exclusive package, the Intergalatic Tour costs $100,000. Don't worry about this large pricetag, though; we offer financing!

Details

During your Intergalatic travel you will:

  • Orbit Mercury, mere miles from the Sun, for a week.
  • Travel to the surface of Venus!
  • Visit the moon for two weeks, visiting Armstrong Station and the Shepard Museum of Lunar Art!
  • Travel to Mars and visit the landing sites of three Mars rovers!
  • Experience our most luxurious spaceship while traveling between planets!
