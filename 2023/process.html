The Process

Interested in taking one of our guided tours? Here's how you can have the opportunity to do so.

The Step-by-Step Process

Getting Started

To get started on your journey to space tourism, there are a few things that must be done before you start.
First, submit a request for information in the portal at the bottom of this page. Once you do this, your request will be reviewed and approved.
You'll then receive information about dates, pricing, liability, and forms to fill out.
You'll then need to fill out the form given to you, verifying your identity, payment information, insurance, and billing information.
After this, you'll be able to move to the next part!

Billing, Payment, and Paperwork Information

After you've finished filling out the form, your information will be reviewed and verified. You'll then finally be all set for the final steps
You'll now need to pick a payment method. Here's what we offer:

  • Upfront payment: One payment all at once
  • Split payment: Multiple payments over one a set amount of time
  • Loans: MUST meet high credit score and yearly income requirements
  • Scholarships!: Are you a student looking to study abroad in space? We offer scholarships to assist you financially if you meet certain requirements.

Shortly after submitting a form of payment, you'll be sent dates for training and launch. We're looking forward to you exploring with us!

To ensure the safety of all of our clients, we require all future explorers to go through a safety training seminar.
In addition to this seminar, all future explorers go through a rigorous six-month-long training regiment. At the end of this regiment, every explorer is required to pass the end-of-course exams to gain flight clearance.
For more information, please visit our training information page.
