The Moon

Overwhelmed by the size, weather, and overall complexity of the galaxy? Then the moon is right for you!

At 1/4th the size of the earth and pretty predictable as far as weather goes, the moon keeps it classic and cool. (Literally! Did you know that the moon can be as cold as -153 Degrees Celcius in areas of shade?). Join us on the Defiant as we travel to the Moon!

Pricing

Worried about how much a trip to the moon costs? A round-trip ticket to the moon and back is only $15,000.

Details

On your trip to the moon you will:

  • Go on a one-hour guided moonwalk across the Kepler Crater!
  • Visit the landing site of Apollo 11!
  • Go shopping in the Mall of the Moon at Armstrong Station!
  • Visit the Alan B. Shepard Museum of Lunar Art!

Mars

Curious about the Red Planet? Mars, our closest celestial neighbor, is a beautiful planet ripe for exploration. As the closest planet in size and shape to our own, Mars is the best planet to visit for new galactic explorers. Join us on the Independence as we make the journey to Mars!

Pricing

This all-inclusive roundtrip to Mars is $40,000.

Details

On your trip to Mars you will:

  • Go on a one-hour, guided expedition across the Columbia Hills complex!
  • Visit the first agricultural center on Mars, where Earth life was first transplanted to another planet!
  • Explore the galaxy's first galactic settlement, New Terra!
  • Go to the Martian meteorite theatre, and watch films about the race to inhabit Mars.

All-Around Intragalactic Tour!

Can't choose which planet you want to visit? Then our Intergalactic Tour is for you! Our intergalactic tour is our most exclusive package. Explorers on this tour will get to experience what life is like on the other three rocky planets of our solar system: Mercury, Venus, and Mars. The intergalactic tour also makes a stop on the moon! Join us on the Archimedes as we tour the galaxy!

Pricing

As befitting of our most exclusive package, the Intergalactic Tour costs $100,000. Don't worry about this large pricetag, though; we offer financing!

Details

During your Intergalactic travel you will:

  • Orbit Mercury, mere miles from the Sun, for a week.
  • Travel to the surface of Venus!
  • Visit the moon for two weeks, visiting Armstrong Station and the Shepard Museum of Lunar Art!
  • Travel to Mars and visit the landing sites of three Mars rovers!
  • Experience our most luxurious spaceship while traveling between planets!

All-New Space Retirement Package, coming 2025

Retirement and setting down for the last years of one's life is a big deal, and sometimes the traditional retirement home route just is not for everyone. If you're looking for adventure as well as the ability to enjoy the comfort of a peaceful lifestyle, then our Space Retirement Plan is for you!
